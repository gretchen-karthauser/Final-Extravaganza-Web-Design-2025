This site is a point-and-click, visual novel style choose your own adventure game I made for my web design class. I took inspiration for the design of the game from the show Gravity Falls, the games Night in the Woods and Dredge, and from the movie Alice in Wonderland. Explore the different pages and all the oddities to be found within them as well as the secret to what's behind the mysterious door if you're clever enough to unlock it. All art, written content, and code for the site were made by Gretchen Karthauser under the instruction and guidence of Andrew McConville.
